## 🚀 Overview

SkyMed Pro is a comprehensive, web-based management system designed for healthcare organizations. It provides end-to-end solutions for vendor management, financial tracking, payroll processing, asset management, and more.

### Key Highlights
- ✅ **100% Client-Side** - Runs entirely in your browser
- ✅ **Offline Capable** - Works without internet connection
- ✅ **Cloud Sync** - Automatic sync with Supabase
- ✅ **Secure** - Built-in security measures
- ✅ **Responsive** - Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile

## ✨ Features

### 📊 Dashboard
- Real-time KPI monitoring
- Contract expiry alerts
- Receivables & Payables summary
- Quick access to all modules

### 🏢 Master Vendor
- Vendor registration with unique codes
- Location/Base management
- Service category mapping
- Rate and GST configuration
- Provision applicability flags

### 📄 Contracts
- Contract lifecycle management
- Addendum, Extension, Termination
- Expiry tracking & alerts
- Contract history & revisions

### 📝 General Entry
- Universal entry system for invoices
- Support for Credit/Debit Notes
- Reference invoice linking
- Workflow status tracking
- Move to Receivables/Payables

### 💰 Finance Module

#### Receivables
- Track incoming payments
- Invoice management
- Status tracking (Pending/Received)
- UTR and payment date tracking

#### Payables
- Vendor payment management
- Invoice processing
- Payment status tracking
- UTR and payment date tracking

#### Advances
- Advance payment tracking
- Security deposit management
- Adjustment history
- Balance monitoring

#### Vendor Ledger
- Complete transaction history
- Debit/Credit tracking
- Running balance per vendor
- Filter by date and type

#### Provision
- Monthly provision tracking
- Financial Year management
- Rollover functionality
- Vendor-wise provision summary

### 👥 Payroll & HR

#### Payroll
- Employee salary management
- Attendance tracking
- Abatement processing
- Salary calculation with extra shifts/hours

#### Employee Management
- Complete employee master data
- Employee contract history
- Status tracking (Active/Resigned/Terminated)

#### Leave Management
- PL, CL, SL tracking
- Leave balance monitoring
- Leave entry and history
- Bulk leave import

### 🔧 Asset Management
- Asset registration (Laptops, Instruments, etc.)
- Assignment tracking
- Issue/Return/Transfer history
- Status tracking

### 💳 Imprest Management
- Employee advance tracking
- Bill submission
- Category-wise expense tracking
- Balance monitoring

### 🗑️ Deleted Records
- Soft delete functionality
- Data recovery
- Permanent deletion

### ⚙️ Advanced Settings
- Custom tab management
- Column configuration
- Custom fields
- Data validation rules

- User preferences
